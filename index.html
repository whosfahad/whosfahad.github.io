Who is the owner of fahadsflicks.com?

Hi everyone! My name is Fahad. Taking photos, for me, is not just about capturing everything. Photos are one of the crucial elements of storytelling. They capture feelings and preserve memories that lets you relive that moment once more. I've been doing photography professionally for about 2 years, but I had a camera in my hand ever since I was a child. I ran a photography club in my high school, and I also published my photos in my school's newspaper. After graduating high school, I'm currently a student at the University of Rochester. Besides becoming a full time programmer, I want to own a studio one day to give the youth photographers a platform to work together and find the audience they deserve.
